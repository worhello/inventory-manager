About Inventory Manager

Inventory Manager stores all user data in the user's browser, under [LocalStorage](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/Window/localStorage).

There are no network calls to outside servers. Your data doesn't go anywhere unless the user (that's you) explicitly exports it onto their own machine.

There is no cloud server. There are no backups. If you clear the storage of your browser, or change browser, the data you've put here won't follow you unless you bring it with you.
